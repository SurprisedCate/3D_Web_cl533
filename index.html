Deeper understanding:

Demonstration of higher 3d modelling complexity. (Candelholder, flower pot and bronze vase).

Used C4D UV edit mode and uv relax option then exported to photoshop to create custom texture for 3d model. (Flower pot, wine jar and candleholder).

Use of a fully functional light mode dark mode through :root and can be integrated with small screen devices through icons.

Realistic render of 3d models with the use of global illumination and ambient occlusion. Tutorial here, here and here.

Inplementation of the lightbox gallery.

Implementation of centered modal.


Issues:

Due to the incompadibility of Cinema 4D's material with X3D, the output X3D model of the bronze vase does not match with the output in Cinema 4D. The original material can be viewed in the webpage 3D images gallery or can be further inspected in the downloadable full 3D file.
